## Низкий LR с лексическими ограничениями

### Пример 1 (Part 2)

**Вопрос:** Describe a gift you received.

**Ответ:**

> I want to talk about a gift I received for my last birthday. It was a beautiful watch from my parents. I had mentioned that I wanted a watch, but I didn't expect them to remember. When I opened the gift, I was really surprised and happy. The watch is not very expensive, but it means a lot to me because it shows that my parents listen to me and care about what I want. I wear it every day now, and every time I look at it, I think of my family. This gift taught me that the value of a gift is not in its price, but in the thought behind it.

**Subscores:**
- Overall: 6.0
- FC (Fluency & Coherence): 7.0
- LR (Lexical Resource): 5.0
- GRA (Grammatical Range & Accuracy): 6.0
- PR (Pronunciation): 6.5
